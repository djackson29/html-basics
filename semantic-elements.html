Movies

Movies are the best!
Some of my favorites are Gladiator, Runaway Bride, Step Brothers, Bridesmaids, and Pride and Prejudice.

TV Shows

TV Shows have more time for character development but often times, they go on too long and the original scope of the show is lost. Shows are also a much longer time commitment than movies.

Conclusions

In conclusion, I love both movies and tv shows.

But I am more likely to commit to a movie than a show.
